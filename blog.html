What is a Veterinarian?

Veterinarian; is the person who takes precautions for the health of all animal species living on earth (pet animals, livestock, exotic animals and national animals), equipped with medical knowledge and legal authority in areas such as diagnosis, treatment, reproduction, health and care of animal diseases. In addition, the prevention and control of patients who can pass from animals to humans, control of animal food are among the duties and responsibilities of veterinarians.

Big Cats Week

Big Cats Week is a project organized by Nat Geo Wild to draw attention to extinct animals and volunteers trying to protect them. The slogan of the meaningful project, the third of which will take place between 3-10 February 2013, is “Roar for Animals”.
